Four key facts about Earth

Earth is the only planet in the solar system known to harbor life.

Water covers roughly 70% of the Earth's surface, making it unique in having water in all three forms: solid, liquid, and gas.

Earth has a solid, rocky surface with a "squishy" interior, featuring a hot inner core of iron and nickel that is roughly as hot as the surface of the sun.

The atmosphere protects the planet from meteors and solar radiation, providing the perfect balance for life.
